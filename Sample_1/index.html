The Bride

Laura

Bernd and I first met at work in 2015. We were co-workers first and then friends. Then at a work happy hour, Bernd had a few too many spicy margaritas and I was tasked with walking him home. Armed with some liquid courage, Bernd expressed his feelings toward me and we went on our first official date the following week. Thank goodness for those spicy margaritas!

Bernd is one of the funniest people I've ever met and he makes me laugh every day. We make everything together, from waiting at the DMV to hiking to the top of Mount Greylock, an exciting and magical experience. I can't wait until we're officially married!

The Groom

Bernd

I would argue that Laura was the one to make the first move. She had been sending me flirty texts for the previous few weeks, so I knew there was something there. And I only had one spicy margarita. Laura was the one to offer to walk me home. Either way, I'm so thankful for that work happy hour and whatever gave me the courage to share how I felt that night.

Laura is so smart, so organized, and so committed to her friends, family, and loved ones. She makes me want to be the best version of myself, whether it's at the gym, cooking, or even just finding the best new show on Netflix. I'm so lucky to have finally found her.

When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Our celebration will take place at the beautiful modern concert hall 'Elbhilharmonie' in Hamburg. The address is Platz d. Deutschen Einheit 4, 20457 Hamburg. There is plenty of parking and it is free. The ceremony will be inside the hall.

Wedding Party

After the wedding ceremony took place, we will have a reception dinner and a kickin' dance party afterwards. The address is the same, Platz d. Deutschen Einheit 4, 20457 Hamburg, but instead of the main hall we will move right next to the ceremony site, just a short walk away.
